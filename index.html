My Favourite Novel

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Also called Omniscient Reader and often abbreviated to ORV is an apocalyptic modern/low fantasy fiction webnovel, written by a Korean author duo writing under the pen name 싱숑, sing N song.

ORV is about a novel called Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World (written by the anonymous author tls123) which has been published over 13 years. Kim Dokja, the sole reader who followed it until the end, suddenly finds the real world mirroring the story. Using his unique knowledge, he allies with Yoo Joonghyuk, the protagonist, to alter the original plot and survive. Together with allies, they face life-or-death challenges while aiming to destroy the Star Stream that demands their suffering.

“If this story can really save you... If you regained just a little of your memories and remember us just one more time... Then I'll keep writing the epilogue for you until the end of time, for eternity.”
- Han Sooyoung
